At some busses you can also use your Pasmo or Suica card but be sure to always have some cash money with you. With busses in the Tokyo 23 wards you have to pay up front to your destination to ride along with the bus. Outside of the 23 wards of Tokyo you can pay after you get to your destination. When you want to travel outside of Tokyo we recommend you take a Highway bus, you can buy a ticket at a highway bus stop or purchase a Japanese bus pass where you can buy for 3, 5 or 7 days. With these passes you can travel unlimited times between those days with a highway bus.
