When Travelling between towns it is easy to you the underground train system. There are 3 kinds of JR-Lines Japan. One for Center-Japan, One for West-Japan and One for East-Japan. These are not the only train lines but the most used. The jr-lines are easy to use and we recommend you get a Pasmo or a Suica card. You can load money on to the card, scan it at the port and pay with it automatically. You have to pay a fee for getting a card but do not worry at the end of your trip you can trade it back or keep it as a souvenir.
You may have noticed the maps above, these are the train guidelines. Do not panick they are easier to use then it may seem. You just search your stop to the stop you want to go to and follow the train lines so you can board the right trains. You can also download some jr-line maps and just put in your locations.
